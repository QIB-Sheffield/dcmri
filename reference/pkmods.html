Classic pharmacokinetic models#

A collection of common pharmacokinetic models in DC-MRI.

One-parameter models#

One-compartment uptake model#

conc_1cum(ca, Fp[, t, dt])

Concentration in a one-compartment uptake model.

flux_1cum(ca, Fp[, t, dt])

Outflux out of a one-compartment uptake model.

Two-parameter models#

One-compartment model#

conc_1cm(ca, Fp, v[, t, dt])

Concentration in a one-compartment model.

flux_1cm(ca, Fp, v[, t, dt])

Outflux out of a one-compartment model.

Tofts model#

conc_tofts(ca, Ktrans, kep[, t, dt, sum])

Concentration in a Tofts model.

flux_tofts(ca, Ktrans, kep[, t, dt])

Outfluxes out of a Tofts model.

Patlak model#

conc_patlak(ca, vp, Ktrans[, t, dt, sum])

Concentration in a Patlak model.

flux_patlak(ca, vp, Ktrans[, t, dt])

Outfluxes out of a 2-compartment uptake model.

Three-parameter models#

Extended Tofts model#

conc_etofts(ca, vp, Ktrans, kep[, t, dt, sum])

Concentration in a two-compartment uptake model.

flux_etofts(ca, vp, Ktrans, kep[, t, dt])

Outfluxes out of an extended Tofts model.

Two-compartment uptake model#

flux_2cum(ca, Fp, vp, PS[, t, dt])

Outfluxes out of a 2-compartment uptake model.
