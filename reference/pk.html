Pharmacokinetic building blocks#

A collection of basic pharmacokinetic models that can be assembled to build more complex models.

No-parameter models#

Trap#

conc_trap(J[, t, dt])

Indicator concentration inside a trap.

flux_trap(J)

Indicator flux out of a trap.

res_trap(t)

Residue function of a trap.

prop_trap(t)

Propagator or transit time distribution of a trap.
